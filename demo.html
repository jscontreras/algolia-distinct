Distinct

In our index configuration, we apply the distinct feature on the model of the product (e.g. "Tshirt A").

It enables us to limit the number of color-variants of a product shown in the search results.

False True 2 3

Distinct is disabled, we display all the variations of the products.

Interesting queries

"Red" with distinct false. "Tshirt" with distinct 3. "Sweat" with distinct 2.
